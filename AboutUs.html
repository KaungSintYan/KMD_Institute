The Story of KMD Group of Companies

KMD was established as a niche computer training institute in 1986.
It was founded by Mr. Thaung Tin and Mrs. Tyn Tyn Aye
KMD was reorganized, restructured and risen in market as a Private Limited Company focused on delivering ICT Solutions.
KMD is the largest one-stop IT Products and Services Provider in Myanmar, with well-established Management and Workforce of 650+
(as on April 2020).
KMD holds large customer base including ongoing relationship with major Government Departments and Private Enterprises
More than 700,000 has been graduated so far from KMD Education
Established as Authorized Distributor in country for major international brands

KMD Group of Companies has been successfully delivering solutions for all technology needs of Industries. With the experience of 30+ years, KMD has established itself as one-stop provider for all IT needs in Myanmar. KMD was started as a training institute and now KMD holds strong presence in IT and Mobile Retail and Distribution, Enterprise Software Solutions, and ICT Training.
